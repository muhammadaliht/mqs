Structure of Online Tafseer Classes

The online Tafseer classes are carefully categorized into six modules. Each module is meant to help students gain a deeper knowledge of the Quran while ensuring a pleasant and complete learning experience.

Module 1: Introduction to Tafseer

The first part of the class is an introduction to the topic of Tafseer, focusing on how important it is to understand the Quran. This module looks at the background of Tafseer and the different ways that scholars have tried to understand the Quran. It also presents the idea of Asbab al-Nuzul, which means "the reasons for revelation." This module gives the verses' historical and social background. By the end of this lesson, students will know what Tafseer is and how it helps us to understand what the Quran is saying.

Module 2: Quranic Recitation and Translation

In this module, students learn how to recite the Quran using the rules of Tajweed correctly. In every class, certain verses are read and translated into English so students can understand their meaning. This lesson helps students link the linguistic aspects of the Quran and its broader message, which is essential for studying Tafseer further.

Module 3: Study of Major Themes

This lesson details the primary concepts in the Quran, which helps you understand its verses better. Some topics are:

Tawheed (Allah's Oneness), Prophethood, and the Hereafter.
Advice on social, moral, and legal issues.
Stories of the Prophets and what we can learn from them.
Students learn more about what the Quran talks about in different parts of life.

Module 4: In-Depth Tafseer

This module explains verses from the Quran in great depth, examining their historical background, subtle differences in language, and subject importance. It is crucial to understand how the message of the Quran relates to the problems we face today. Students are told stories about the Prophets, moral lessons, and advice on personal and societal issues. This module helps students understand the wisdom and importance of the teachings in the Quran by giving them clarity and meaning.

Module 5: Reflection and Application

This module focuses on applying the Quran's teachings to practice. Students are asked to think about important topics such as faith, morals, justice, and the well-being of the community. Through homework and class discussions, they look at how to follow the Quran's advice in their personal and social lives. This way of thinking ensures that the information you learn is useful and changes you.

Module 6: Review and Certification

This last module reviews and synthesizes everything covered so far. Students attend review classes, interactive QnA sessions, and tests to ensure they understand better. At the end of this section, participants are graded on how well they know Tafseer. They are given a certificate to show they have completed the online Tafseer course and are committed to understanding the Quran.

Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced Levels in Online Tafseer Classes

Online Tafseer classes are often divided into beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. This arrangement allows students to learn at their own pace and level of comfort. Each level builds progressively, ensuring a seamless learning journey for all participants.

Beginner Level

Students at the beginner level learn about the structure, themes, and importance of knowing the Quran's message. The focus of Tafseer classes is on getting excellent at reciting with correct Tajweed (pronunciation) and learning basic translations. It talks about the Tafseer of shorter Surahs, mostly from Juz Amma (the 30th part of the Quran), focusing on their primary concepts and valuable lessons. This level is perfect for people new to studying Islam or who don't know much about Arabic and Tafseer. The goal is to teach kids about the most important parts of the Quran and Tafseer

Intermediate Level

At the middle level, students do more than just reading and translating. The emphasis changes to understanding the Quran's language beauty, thematic coherence, and historical context. During tafseer classes, students learn more about the middle-length Surahs, like those in Juz Tabarak Allazee (the 29th part) and other parts of the Quran. The main goal is to gain a better understanding and learn more about the meaning of the Quran.

A deeper analysis of Asbab al-Nuzul (reasons for revelation).
Lessons from stories of the Prophets (Qasas al-Anbiya).
Exploration of ethical, social, and legal rulings (Ahkaam).

Advanced Level

At the advanced level, students explore the whole Quran, focusing on deep linguistic analysis and complex topics. The goal is to fully understand the Quran and become an expert in Tafseer methodologies. What's in this level:

Tafseer of longer and more complicated Surahs, like “Surah An-Nisa, Surah’’ Al-Baqarah, and Surah Al-Imran, is learned.
Studying old and new Tafseer books, such as “Tafseer Ibn Kathir, Al-Qurtubi, Asan Tarjuma Quran by Mufti Taqi Usmani”, etc.
Analyzing Tafseer's approaches and interpretations in comparison.
It talks about how to clear up misunderstandings about Islam and the Quran.

As students improve at a certain level, they can advance to the next level. Each level is designed to be adaptable, allowing students to review previous lessons or go at their own pace based on their learning needs. This step-by-step strategy ensures students understand the Quran's message based on their background and learning goals.
