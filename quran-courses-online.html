Overview

At Muhammadan Quran School, we carefully create our online Quran courses. We want to help every student who wishes to learn more about the Quran and Sunnah.

Muhammadan Quran School offers a variety of different online Quran courses. We cover numerous topics to help all our students, regardless of their level of Quranic knowledge. For those just starting, we have foundational courses. Starting from beginner classes, like Noorani Qaida, Basic Quran reading, and Salah & Kalmay, they focus on the basics of Arabic and how to recite the Quran with Tajweed. Also, there are Islamic duas and other important Islamic concepts, such as Quran recitation with Tajweed and techniques for memorization.

As students progress, they get to explore more complex subjects like Tafsir. Tafsir is about interpreting and understanding the Quranic verses. There’s also Tajweed, which helps with saying the words correctly. Online Quran courses are designed to teach the recitation of the Quran with Tajweed in minimum time.

We don’t stop there! More specialized online courses cover subjects like Quranic sciences and Islamic studies. We also teach daily Islamic practices, such as Salah or Namaz, Kalimay, and prayers. Our goal is to give students a well-rounded experience in learning the Quran.
