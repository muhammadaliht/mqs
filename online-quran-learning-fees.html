Refund Policy

Our refund policy is designed to ensure fairness and transparency. If you are not satisfied with the plan you have chosen, please contact our admin team before your classes begin. We will review your request and provide a full refund if it meets our refund criteria. Please note that refunds may not be available in certain cases. For example, once classes have started, the fee becomes non-refundable.

We Offer a Full Refund

The Messenger of Allah (blessings and peace of Allah be upon him) said: "Whoever accepts the offer of a Muslim (buyer) (i.e., takes back the thing he bought) from him, Allah will forgive his mistake on the Day of Resurrection." Therefore, if a student enrolls in a course and pays the fee, but for some reason, he/she cannot attend the classes and wants to request a refund, the full fee will be refunded. Even if the refund is requested after one month, the full fee will still be refunded.
