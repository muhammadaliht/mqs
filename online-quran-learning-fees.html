Refund Policy

Our refund policy is designed to ensure fairness and transparency. If you are unsatisfied with your chosen plan, don't hesitate to contact our admin team before your classes begin. We Offer a Full Refund

The Messenger of Allah (blessings and peace of Allah be upon him) said: "Whoever accepts the offer of a Muslim (buyer) (i.e., takes back the thing he bought) from him, Allah will forgive his mistake on the Day of Resurrection." Therefore, if a student enrolls in a course and pays the fee but, for some reason, cannot attend the classes and wants to request a refund, the full payment will be refunded. Even if the refund is requested after one month, the full fee will still be refunded.
