Our Background and Founding Story

Muhammadan Qur'an School is a trusted online platform for Quran lessons founded in 2017. It is run under the supervision of highly experienced management. Experienced and fluent English and Arabic-speaking male and female Quran teachers guide the students.

This online Quran learning platform does not represent or support any particular group or sect. Its sole purpose is to convey the Quran and the teachings of the Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) to the whole world.

The platform's founder, Hafiz Muhammad Hammad, who desired to replace traditional learning with modern teaching methods, created an online platform for learning the Quran under expert supervision. Since its inception, Muhammadan Quran School has become a global and truly special platform.

With a caring team of male & female Quran tutors (Qari, Qarias), it aims to create a pleasant environment for all students. A supportive team and unique teaching methods will increase your Quran and Hadith knowledge here. Our welcoming space attracts Quran enthusiasts from worldwide!
